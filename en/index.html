Why Chromission?

Do you have a passion for photography but often freeze up wondering what to shoot? Chromission was built for exactly those moments of hesitation.

  • 🤔

    No more shutter paralysis

    A fresh theme every day dissolves the "what do I shoot?" moment so you can just press the shutter.

  • 🎯

    Purpose-driven shooting

    Each day's "mission" trains you to see ordinary subjects in a new light, turning the act of shooting itself into a game.

  • 📔

    Visualize your memories

    Bring the day's photo fragments together into a single board — a rich archive that lets you relive the mood and moments of any given day.

Core Features

  • 🎯

    Daily Mission

    Every day you receive a random theme from five categories: Color, Object, Place, Brand, and Typography. The mission card "pops out" like a Polaroid printer — a visual metaphor that makes each reveal a little treat. Not feeling the mission? Watch an ad and re-roll once.

    🎨 Color 🍕 Object 📍 Place 🏷️ Brand ✏️ Typography
  • 🧩

    9-Slot Grid Collection

    Shoot with the in-app camera or pick from your album to fill the grid. You don't have to fill all 9 slots — wrap up whenever you're ready.

  • ⏰

    Your Day, Your Schedule

    Set your own daily cut-off time to match your lifestyle. Night owls and early birds can both record at their own rhythm.

  • ✨

    Free Editing

    Rearrange photos in the grid and apply light adjustments before saving. Even after saving, one more edit opportunity is provided.

Your Daily Flow

  • TODAY'S MISSION

    Check today's mission card. Not feeling it? Watch an ad and re-roll once.

  • MY SOURCE 0 / 9

    Each photo you add from the camera or album fills the 9-slot grid in real time.

  • Wrap Up & Edit

    When all 9 slots are filled the day closes automatically, or you can close it manually and arrange the photos in the edit screen.

  • MY POST

    Completed grids stack up by date in MY POST on your profile page. Posts in progress live in the IN PROGRESS area; finished posts appear separately in the grid view for a clean overview.

App Permissions

The permissions below are used solely to power Chromission's core features and are never used for advertising or marketing identification.

  • Camera

    Used to take mission photos.

  • Photos & Media

    Used to select photos from your album and add them to the grid.

  • Notifications (optional)

    Used for alerts related to your photo mission flow — new mission arrival, upcoming deadline, etc. Denying this permission does not affect app usage.

  • App Tracking (ATT, iOS)

    A system prompt shown to obtain consent for use of the advertising identifier (IDFA). Denying does not restrict app usage in any way.
